About

With 4 years of programming experience, I have acquired a wide range of languages and technologies. I have studied C++, C, and C#, and have also self-taught Python and Dart/Flutter. Utilizing these skills, I have tackled various projects.

In Python-based projects, I developed a household budget app, a password management app, and a web portfolio using the Django framework. Additionally, I created a handwritten character recognition app by leveraging machine learning technology.

In Dart/Flutter development, I created a database access control app. Through this experience, I have been able to improve my development capabilities across different platforms and environments.

I have actively participated in team projects, developing file-sharing apps and household budget apps. This has allowed me to hone my team development skills and acquire the ability to collaborate with other members to advance projects. My diverse technical expertise and teamwork will surely be put to good use in future projects.
